Mac and Cheese

What is Mac and Cheese?

Mac and Cheese is a dish consisting of cooked macaroni pasta combined with a cheese sauce, generally being cheddar. It is typically prepared as a baked good, or on the stovetop for a saucier version.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Optionally: bread crumbs and flour

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles, drain them, and then transfer them to a baking dish
  2. Make the cheese sauce, pour the sauce over the noodles, then stir
  3. Make the topping, spread it over macaroni and cheese, and sprinkle with paprika
  4. Bake the mac and cheese at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes or until the topping is a golden brown
