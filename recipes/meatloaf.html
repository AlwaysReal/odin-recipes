Meatloaf

What is Meatloaf?

Meatloaf is an American dish made by mixing grounded meat such as beef with spice. It generally is combined with onions, garlic, breadcrumbs, and eggs.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Vegetables
  • Egg
  • Spices
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Optionally: Sauces and glaze

Steps

  1. Take your vegetables and chop them up
  2. Combine the vegetables, bread crumbs, spices, eggs, and sauces with your grounded meat and mix them together.
  3. Form the meat mixture into a loaf and place it in a baking pan, then bake for about 15 minutes

Note about baking time

Baking times for meatloafs will vary based on size of the baking pan you use. You want the internal temperature of the meat to be 160 degrees F, this recipe usually calls for around 60-75 minutes for baking time.
