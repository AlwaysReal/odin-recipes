Shepherd's Pie

What is a Shepherd's Pie?

The Shepherd's Pie is a classic British casserole consisting of ground beef or lamb mixed with peas, carrots, and other vegetables and topped off with mashed potatoes. It is then baked until the meat is bubbling and the potates are golden.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef (or Ground Lamb)
  • Mashed Potatoes
  • Vegetables (Carrots, Peas, Onions)
  • Flour
  • Optionally: Cheese, Ketchup or Tomato Paste, Garlic, Garnishes

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Add potatoes into the pot and cook until tender but still firm, approximately 15 minutes. Drain and mash the potatoes, and mix in optional ingredients such as chopped onion, shredded cheese, and butter before setting aside.
  2. Boil another pot of salted water and add carrots. Cook for another 15 minutes, aiming for the same tender but firm feeling as the potatoes. Drain them, mash them, and then set them aside and preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  3. Grab a frying pan and put it on the stove to begin cooking your ground beef. Cook until it is well browned, pour out excess fat, then stir in flour and cook for another minute. Optionally add ketchup and beef broth here, then reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Spread the ground beef evenly at the bottom of a 2 quart casserole dish. Spread the carrots over it, then top it all off with mashed potatoes. You can mix remaining shredded cheese at your discretion.
  5. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes, or until the mashed potatoes are golden. Enjoy!
